About Me

Certified AEM software developer with 7 years of experience of work on AEM projects. Deep knowledge of AEM and related technologies. Full-stack development from designing a master template to bug-fixing in the created components. My range of tasks and abilities include AEM developing both on the backend (Java) and on the frontend (JS/HTML/HTL). Play a Key Developer role and provide support for teammates and knowledge sharing. Effective discussions and e-mail threads, able to promptly clarify complex issues.
